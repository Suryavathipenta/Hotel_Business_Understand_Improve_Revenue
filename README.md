# Hotel_Business_Understand_Improve_Revenue
Power BI project focused on hotel business analysis using star schema modeling, data transformation, and revenue insights.
# Hotel Business: Understand and Improve Revenue

## Dataset
- **hotel_data.csv** (located in Data folder)
- Contains booking details, room information, guest type, revenue, occupancy, etc.

## Objective
- Analyze hotel revenue and occupancy trends.
- Understand booking patterns by season, room category, and guest type.
- Provide insights for business improvements.

## Methodology
- **Data Transformation**: Performed in Power Query.
- **Fact Table**: Bookings
- **Dimension Tables**: Date, Room, Customer, Hotel Branches
- **Calculated Columns**:
  - Booking Duration
  - Room Category
  - Stay Type (Short / Medium / Long)
- **Star Schema**: Built with Fact Table at the center and Dimension Tables connected.

## Key Visualizations
- **Total Revenue**
- **Room Revenue by Season and Room Category**
- **Revenue by Guest Type**
- **Occupancy Rate by Season**
- Screenshots of visualizations are in `Screenshots` folder.

## Outcome
- Identify revenue trends and occupancy patterns.
- Insights for optimizing hotel performance and room management.
- Shows business areas where revenue can be improved and where occupancy is high or low.
